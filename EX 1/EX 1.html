Garlic Honey Chicken

This Garlic Honey Chicken recipe is crispy, flavorful, and coated in a rich, sweet, and savory sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg Boneless chicken cubes
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tbsp Garlic powder/paste

Flour Mix:

  • 2 cups Plain flour
  • 1 cup Corn flour
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tbsp Chilli powder
  • 1 tbsp Black Pepper
  • 1 tbsp Oregano
  • 1 tbsp Ginger powder
  • 1 tsp Garlic powder
  • Oil (for frying)

Sauce:

  • 150g Butter
  • 2 bulbs of Garlic (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup Soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup Sugar
  • 1/3 cup Honey

Method

  1. Marinate the chicken cubes with salt and garlic powder. Let them sit for 30 minutes.
  2. In a bowl, mix all the flour mix ingredients together.
  3. Separate half of the flour mix into another bowl.
  4. In one of the bowls, add 250ml of water and mix to create a wet batter.
  5. Take the marinated chicken and dip it in the wet batter.
  6. Then coat it in the dry flour mix.
  7. Repeat the process: Wet batter → Dry flour → Wet batter → Dry flour for a crispier texture.
  8. Heat oil in a pan and deep-fry the coated chicken until golden brown and crispy.
  9. In another pan, melt butter and sauté the chopped garlic.
  10. Add soy sauce, sugar, and honey, then stir well.
  11. Add the fried chicken to the sauce and mix until evenly coated.
  12. Serve hot and enjoy.
