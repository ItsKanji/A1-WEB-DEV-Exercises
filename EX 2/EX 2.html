FRIENDS

"The One Where Everyone Loves FRIENDS"

Overview

FRIENDS is a beloved sitcom that aired from 1994 to 2004. It follows the lives of six friends—Rachel, Ross, Monica, Chandler, Joey, and Phoebe—as they navigate relationships, careers, and hilarious everyday situations in New York City.

Key Information

  • Genre: Sitcom, Comedy, Romance
  • Created by: David Crane & Marta Kauffman
  • Number of Seasons: 10
  • Number of Episodes: 236
  • Original Network: NBC

Cast

  • Jennifer Aniston as Rachel Green
  • Courteney Cox as Monica Geller
  • Lisa Kudrow as Phoebe Buffay
  • Matt LeBlanc as Joey Tribbiani
  • Matthew Perry as Chandler Bing
  • David Schwimmer as Ross Geller

Main Characters

Rachel Green: A fashion enthusiast who starts as a waitress and later works in the fashion industry.

Ross Geller: A paleontologist known for his three divorces and "We were on a break!" debate.

Monica Geller: A perfectionist chef and Ross's sister who is obsessed with cleanliness.

Chandler Bing: The king of sarcasm with an undefined office job and later a family man.

Joey Tribbiani: A struggling actor with a love for food and the phrase "How you doin'?"

Phoebe Buffay: A quirky musician known for her song "Smelly Cat."
